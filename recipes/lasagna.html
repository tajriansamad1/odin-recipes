Lasagna Recipe

Description: Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a favorite for many. It is a pasta dish made by alternating layers of cheese, sauce, and meat. The dish is then baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Lasagna is a hearty and comforting dish that is perfect for a family dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients:

For the meat sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)

For the ricotta mixture:

  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley or 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • A pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the meat sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat.
    • Add the onions and sauté until translucent (about 3 minutes). Add garlic and cook for another minute.
    • Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and chili flakes. Simmer for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Taste and adjust seasoning. Add sugar if needed.
  3. Prepare the ricotta mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, parsley, nutmeg (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles (if using regular noodles):
    • Boil the noodles in salted water according to the package instructions. Drain and lay them flat on a clean towel to prevent sticking.
  5. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Lay 3 noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread a third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Spoon a layer of meat sauce on top and sprinkle with mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella) two more times.
    • Finish with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake:
    • Cover the dish with aluminum foil (avoid touching the cheese).
    • Bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 20–25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  7. Rest and serve:
    • Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before cutting. This helps it hold its shape when served.
